About Me
Ahmedabad based artist, Khushi prajapati, has been honing her skills in painting masterpeices for the past several years. Originally a sketch artist, Khushi expanded on to canvas painting bringing out her wonderful talent for art.

Khushi's jorney into art allowed her to understand herself more. Her connection with her art is something one can not achieve unless they have a deep passion for it Her art inspires her to do more and gives out a sense of peace and that is what is seen in her paintings.
